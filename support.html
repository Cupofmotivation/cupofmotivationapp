Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Cup of Motivation?
Cup of Motivation is an app designed to inspire and motivate you in your daily life.
2. How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
3. Can I use the app offline?
Yes, you can access certain features of the app offline.
4. How do I contact customer support?
You can contact customer support via the email addresses listed above.
5. Are there any system requirements for the app?
The app requires an internet connection and is available on both iOS and Android devices.
6. How can I give feedback about the app?
You can send your feedback to the feedback email listed above.
7. What should I do if I find a bug?
Please report any bugs to the bugs email listed above.
8. Where can I find documentation for the app?
You can find documentation on our website under the "Documentation" section.
