# Cup of Motivation - School App

Welcome to Cup of Motivation, a student-focused application designed to help you stay motivated and productive throughout your school day.

## 📱 About the App

Cup of Motivation is a comprehensive platform that provides daily motivation, study support, and goal-tracking features for students of all ages.

### Key Features

- **📚 Study Support** - Daily motivation and study tips
- **🎯 Goal Tracking** - Set and track personal goals
- **👥 Community** - Connect with other students
- **🔔 Reminders** - Timely notifications to stay on track
